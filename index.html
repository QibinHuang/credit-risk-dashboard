Credit Risk Analysis and Fraud Detection Dashboard

Built with Power BI, SQL, and Python.
Simulated 2M+ credit records for risk scoring, fraud detection, and insight visualization.

Overview Fraud Heatmap Model Metrics

1. Overview Dashboard

Visualizes total loan volume, fraud case count, average interest rate, and risk breakdown by credit score group.

2. Fraud Detection Heatmap

Heatmap shows geographic fraud density and risk level by state, highlighting regional anomalies.

3. Model Performance (ROC & Precision-Recall)

Model comparison using AUC and precision-recall, with Random Forest and XGBoost achieving top fraud detection performance.
